Let me introduce myself briefly:
I have a PhD degree in Interdisciplinary Information Science (IIS) from the University of North Texas (UNT), and worked in Turkish National Police (TNP) in various capacities, including forensics and information systems. I also have a significant international experience in UN missions; East Timor, Bosnia, and New York. I am certified as a police instructor, a forensic expert, a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE), and taught in-service trainings, forensics, and criminal justice related courses.
